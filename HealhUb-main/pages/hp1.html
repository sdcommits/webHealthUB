Hospital Information

Manipal Hospital

About: Manipal Hospitals is 380 bedded multi-specialty healthcare providers catering to both Indian and international patients. It is one of India’s foremost hospitals that aim at providing specialized tertiary and quaternary care. Manipal hospital is a part of the Manipal Education and Medical Group (MEMG) – a leader in the areas of education and healthcare. With more than 5000 operational beds, their hospitals provide quality and affordable healthcare to everyone

History: Manipal hospital, Dwarka is spread over 5,60,000 square feet and houses 7 Centers of Excellence in , Orthopedics & Joint Replacements, Renal Sciences- Nephrology & Urology, Cardiac Sciences, Medical and Surgical Gastroenterology, Neuro Sciences, Oncology Services, Gynecology and Pediatrics with cutting edge technology, state of the art equipment and advanced support services for enhanced patient outcomes. Manipal group operates a chain of multi-specialty hospitals with over 5200 beds and has footprints across Salem, Goa, Bangalore, Manipal, Mangalore, Vijayawada, and Jaipur. Manipal Hospital Dwarka is the group’s first hospital in the capital and tenth in the country. The group also operates a facility in Klang, Malaysia

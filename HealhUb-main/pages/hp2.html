Hospital Information

Max Hospital

About: One of the leading Healthcare institutes Max Healthcare Institute is a hospital chain based in New Delhi, India with 14 hospitals across North India committed to providing quality medical care to the community

History: Max Super Specialty Hospital in Saket was founded in 2006 and established as a part of the Max Healthcare consortium and a unit of Devki Devi Foundation and listed in the Bombay Stock Exchange and National Stock Exchange with more than 37,000 shareholders. Max is a public limited company and several of its hospitals are NABH Accredited.
